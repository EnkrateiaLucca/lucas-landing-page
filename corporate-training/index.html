    How It Works¶

    1. Discovery call (20 min) - Understand your team's goals, current capabilities, and specific challenges

    2. Custom proposal - Tailored agenda, exercises using your actual workflows, and materials for your context

    3. Pre-workshop prep - Environment setup instructions, prerequisite resources, and participant onboarding

    4. Workshop delivery - Interactive, hands-on training with real-time Q&A (remote or on-site in Europe)

    5. Follow-up support - Q&A access period for implementation questions (14-30 days depending on program)

    Frequently Asked Questions¶

    Can workshops be customized to our tech stack?

    Yes. For technical workshops, we adapt code examples and exercises to your team's languages, frameworks, and tools. The core concepts remain the same, but implementation details match your environment.

    Do you offer on-site delivery?

    Yes. On-site delivery is available throughout Europe. Travel expenses are additional to the workshop fee. Virtual delivery via Zoom is also available with no timezone restrictions within reason.

    What's the ideal group size?
    • Engineering workshop: 5-15 participants for optimal hands-on support
    • Productivity workshop: Up to 20 participants
    • Executive briefing: 5-10 participants for interactive discussion
    Can you train multiple teams or run recurring sessions?

    Yes. For organizations training multiple teams, I offer package pricing and can schedule recurring sessions. Contact me to discuss your needs.
